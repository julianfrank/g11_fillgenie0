General Guidelines

Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) zones are determined by the interconnecting utility's nodal pricing map. For the purpose of this application:

  • Zone A (Premium): CAISO SP-15, PJM Dominion, ISO-NE Boston.
  • Zone B (Standard): ERCOT North, NYISO Zone J, MISO Hub.
  • Zone C (Emerging): All other non-regulated markets.

Please refer to the daily ISO spot price report if you are unsure of the current classification.

At a minimum, we require:
  1. Last 2 years of audited financial statements (or tax returns for SPVs).
  2. Preliminary PVSyst production report (P50 and P90 probability).
  3. Site control evidence (Lease option or Title deed).
  4. Single Line Diagram (SLD) stamped by a PE if system size > 500kW.

Regulatory Compliance

Project eligibility for the 30% ITC (and potential 10% adders) depends on:

1. Prevailing Wage & Apprenticeship (PWA): Must be met for projects > 1MW AC.
2. Domestic Content: 10% adder if 100% of steel/iron and 40% of manufactured products are US-made.
3. Energy Community: 10% adder for brownfields or coal-closure census tracts.
Note: We strictly audit PWA requirements. Failure to provide certified payrolls will result in loan default.

A UCC-1 financing statement must be filed in the state where the asset is located AND the state of incorporation of the borrower. We require a first-position lien on the solar asset and a pledge of equity in the SPV.

Technical Glossary

Merchant Curve

The projected forward curve of wholesale electricity prices in the specific ISO node. Used to value uncontracted revenue streams.

Degradation Rate

The annual percentage loss of module efficiency. We assume 0.5% per annum unless specific module datasheets (e.g. REC Alpha Pure) warrant a lower assumption (0.25%).

DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio)

Net Operating Income / Total Debt Service. Minimum requirement for underwriting is 1.25x for contracted cashflows and 1.5x for merchant cashflows.

Interconnection Queue

The waiting list for connecting new generation to the grid. 'Queue Position' is a critical determinant of project timeline risk.
